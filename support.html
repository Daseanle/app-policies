Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync data to my Apple Watch?
Make sure both your iPhone and Apple Watch apps are open. Tap the "Sync Now" button on your Watch. The data should appear instantly. Note: iCloud sync happens automatically in the background.
Is my data private?
Yes, absolutely. All your health data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud. We do not have access to it.
How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases."
