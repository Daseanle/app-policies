Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

1. Overview

ResetLife AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by ResetLife AI.

2. Data Collection and Storage

ResetLife AI is a privacy-first application. All your journal entries, goals, and personal "Vision" data are stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud container (via CloudKit). We do not have access to your data.

3. FaceID and Biometrics

We use FaceID (biometric authentication) solely for the purpose of locking the application to protect your privacy. Biometric data is processed locally by the specialized hardware on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

4. Analytics

We do not collect personally identifiable analytics. We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data to improve app performance (e.g., crash reports via Apple's standard opt-in system).
