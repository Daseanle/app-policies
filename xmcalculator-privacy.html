Smart Tape Calculator - Privacy Policy

Privacy First: We believe a calculator should be a private tool. Coco Calculator does not collect, track, or sell your personal data.

1. Data Collection

We operate on a "what happens on your device, stays on your device" policy.

  • Calculation History: Stored locally on your iPhone/iPad using SwiftData. We have no access to it.
  • Voice Data: The app uses the system's text-to-speech engine. The app does not record your voice.
  • Location: We do not request location access.

2. Internet Usage

The app accesses the internet only for the following purpose:

  • Currency Rates: To fetch the latest exchange rates from open financial APIs. This process is anonymous.

3. Third-Party Services

The app utilizes standard Apple frameworks (SwiftUI, SwiftData, AVFoundation). It does not integrate with any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Mixpanel) or advertising networks.
