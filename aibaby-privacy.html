1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Future Baby Peek ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Photos You Upload

When you use AI Future Baby Peek, you upload photos of parents to generate baby predictions. These photos are:

  • Transmitted securely via encrypted connection (HTTPS)
  • Processed by our AI engine
  • Automatically deleted within 24 hours after generation
  • Never stored permanently on our servers
  • Never shared with third parties

2.2 Device Information

We may collect basic device information for app functionality:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs (anonymous)

2.3 Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate AI baby predictions based on uploaded photos
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Temporary Storage: Photos are only stored temporarily during processing
  • Automatic Deletion: All uploaded photos are automatically deleted within 24 hours
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not maintain a database of user photos

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • AI Processing: Replicate.com or similar AI platforms (photos are deleted after processing)

  • Payment Processing: Apple App Store (we do not access payment details)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we process

AI Future Baby Peek is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. The app generates fictional baby images for entertainment purposes only.

8. Data Retention
