Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

HealthStack+ ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your data when you use our application.

1. Data Collection & Storage

We believe your health data belongs to you. HealthStack+ does not store your personal health data on any external servers managed by us.

All data regarding your supplements, schedules, and check-ins is stored locally on your device and may be synchronized across your own devices using Apple's iCloud services (CloudKit). We do not have access to this data.

2. App Permissions

To provide specific features, we may request access to:

  • Camera & Photo Library: Used strictly to allow you to scan supplement labels or attach photos to your supplement entries. These images never leave your device/iCloud ecosystem.
  • Notifications: Used to send you local reminders for your supplement schedule.
  • Bluetooth (Optional): Used only if pairing with specific health accessories.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to improve our app:

  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions and purchase receipts. RevenueCat handles secure validation of transactions. They collect anonymous identifiers to link your purchase to your device.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
