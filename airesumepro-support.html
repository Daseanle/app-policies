Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my resume?

Once you have generated your resume content using AI, tap the "Generate Resume" button, then tap the "Export PDF" button at the top right of the preview screen.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your personal data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your resume on our servers.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscriptions in your iPhone Settings -> Apple ID -> Subscriptions.
