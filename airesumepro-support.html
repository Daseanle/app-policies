Having trouble? We're here to help you build the perfect resume.

How do I export my resume?

After generating your resume content, tap the 'Generate Resume' button. Once generated, an 'Export PDF' button will appear at the top right of the preview card.

Is my resume data private?

Yes, absolutely. Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not keep copies of your resumes on our servers.

How do I verify a purchase?

If you purchased credits but don't see them, tap the 'Restore Purchases' button on the paywall screen.
