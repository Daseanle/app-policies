Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

AI Resume Pro ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII). All resume data (name, email, phone, experience) is processed locally on your device or temporarily sent to our AI provider (OpenRouter/DeepSeek) solely for the purpose of generating content. We do not save this data on our servers.

2. AI Processing

To provide resume generation features, user inputs are sent to third-party AI service providers. These providers are not permitted to use your data for training their models.

3. Local Storage

Your resume data is stored locally on your device. You have full control over your data and can delete it by reinstalling the app or clearing app data.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@airesumepro.app
