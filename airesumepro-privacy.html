Privacy Policy

Your Privacy comes First. We built AI Resume Pro to be a private, secure tool. We DO NOT store your generated resumes on our servers. Your career data belongs to you.

1. Data Collection

We believe in data minimization. Here is what we collect:

  • Input Data: The text you input (job title, experience, skills) is processed temporarily to generate your resume content. It is NOT used to train our AI models.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics (e.g., number of resumes generated) to help us improve app stability.

2. AI Processing

To provide high-quality resume suggestions, we transmit anonymized text data to our AI provider (OpenRouter/DeepSeek). This data is:

  • Encrypted in transit (HTTPS).
  • Not retained by the AI provider for model training.
  • Transient: Only used for the duration of the request.

3. Local Storage

All your finalized resume data (PDFs, saved profiles) is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage sandboxing.
