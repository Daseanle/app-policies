Privacy Policy

Privacy First: We believe your photos are sacred. AI Headshot Pro does not store, sell, or share your personal photos.

1. Data Collection

We operate on a strict "process and delete" policy.

  • Photos You Upload: Temporarily processed via encrypted AI servers and automatically deleted within 24 hours of generation completion.
  • Generated Headshots: Stored locally on your device only. We do not keep copies on our servers.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Apple. We never see your credit card details.
  • Device Data: We collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g., which styles are popular) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your uploaded photos are used solely for AI headshot generation:

  • AI Processing: Photos are sent to our secure AI servers (encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS 1.3).
  • Style Application: AI analyzes facial features and applies the selected professional style.
  • Delivery: Generated headshots are delivered back to your device.
  • Deletion: Original photos and processing data are permanently deleted from our servers within 24 hours.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • AI Processing Partner: Replicate.com (or similar) for AI model inference. They are contractually bound to delete your data after processing.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data only (no photos, no personal info).

4. Data Security

  • All photo uploads are encrypted using industry-standard TLS 1.3.
  • AI servers are SOC 2 compliant and GDPR-ready.
  • No photos are stored in plain text or accessible to our team.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of any data we hold (though we don't store photos).
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account data at any time.
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

AI Headshot Pro is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL and within the app.
