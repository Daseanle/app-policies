How does AI Baby work?

AI Baby uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze facial features from both parents' photos and predict what your future baby might look like. Simply upload one photo of each parent, choose a style, and let our AI generate adorable baby predictions!

Is the prediction accurate?

AI Baby is designed for entertainment purposes. While our AI uses sophisticated facial feature analysis, predictions are not scientifically accurate and should be enjoyed as fun, creative content.

What photos should I upload?

For best results:

  • Use clear, front-facing photos
  • Ensure good lighting
  • Face should be clearly visible
  • Avoid sunglasses or face coverings

How long does generation take?

AI processing typically takes 20-30 seconds. You'll receive 20+ baby prediction photos once complete.

Can I save and share the results?

Yes! You can save all generated baby photos to your device and share them on social media.

What about my privacy?

We take privacy seriously. All uploaded photos are:

  • Encrypted during transmission
  • Processed securely
  • Automatically deleted after generation
  • Never shared with third parties

To cancel:

  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
