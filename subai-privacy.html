SubAI is a privacy-first app. All your subscription data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or sell any of your personal information. Period.

1. Information We Collect

Short Answer: None.

SubAI does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All subscription information you enter into the app is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework.

What Data Stays on Your Device:

  • Subscription names, amounts, and due dates
  • Notes and reminders you create
  • App settings and preferences
  • Custom background images (if you upload them)

2. iCloud Sync (Optional)

SubAI offers optional iCloud synchronization to backup your data and sync across your Apple devices. If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your subscription data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
  • We (the developers) do not have access to your iCloud data
  • Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy applies to synced data
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí SubAI

3. Permissions We Request

SubAI may request the following optional permissions:

Notifications

Purpose: To send you payment reminders and cancellation alerts.
Data: Notification content is generated locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers.

Face ID / Touch ID

Purpose: To lock the app and protect your subscription data.
Data: Biometric data never leaves your device and is managed entirely by Apple's Secure Enclave.

Photo Library (Optional)

Purpose: To allow you to set custom background images.
Data: Selected images are stored locally on your device only.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

SubAI does not integrate with:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase
  • Facebook SDK
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your subscription information is protected by:

  • iOS device encryption (when your device is locked)
  • Apple's Secure Enclave (for Face ID/Touch ID)
  • iCloud encryption (if you enable sync)

We recommend enabling a device passcode and Face ID/Touch ID for maximum security.

6. Children's Privacy

SubAI is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, this app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)

Even though we don't collect data, we want to be transparent about your rights:

  • Right to Access: All your data is already accessible to you in the app
  • Right to Delete: You can delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in iOS Settings
  • Right to Export: You can export your data to CSV from the app's Settings
  • Right to Opt-Out: Since we don't collect data, there's nothing to opt out of

8. International Data Transfers

Since SubAI does not transmit any data to our servers, there are no international data transfers. If you use iCloud sync, your data is stored in Apple's iCloud infrastructure according to your Apple ID region.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We will notify you of significant changes through an in-app notification.
