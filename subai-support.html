‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I add a new subscription?

A: Tap the blue "+" button at the bottom of the home screen. Select a category (AI Tools, Streaming, etc.), enter the name, amount, and due date. SubAI will automatically calculate your yearly spending.

Q2: Can I track non-AI subscriptions?

A: Absolutely! While SubAI is optimized for AI services, you can track any subscription: Netflix, Spotify, gym memberships, cloud storage, and more.

Q3: How do reminders work?

A: SubAI sends you a notification 3 days before each subscription payment is due. You can also set custom "cancel reminders" for trial subscriptions to avoid unwanted charges.

Q4: Is my data private?

A: Yes! All your subscription data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. We use optional iCloud sync for backup, but we never see or access your data.

Q5: Does SubAI support multiple currencies?

A: Yes! SubAI automatically uses your device's regional currency settings. You can enter amounts in any currency, and the app will display them correctly.

Q6: How do I delete a subscription?

A: Tap on any subscription card, then tap the "..." menu button in the bottom right. Select "Delete" from the menu. The subscription will be moved to trash (you can restore it from Settings if needed).

Q7: Can I export my data?

A: Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí Export to CSV. You can then open the file in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app.

Q8: What's the "Yearly Projection" feature?

A: SubAI automatically calculates how much each subscription costs you per year based on its billing cycle (weekly, monthly, quarterly, or yearly). This helps you see the true cost of your subscriptions.
